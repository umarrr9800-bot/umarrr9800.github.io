Privacy Policy — SpikeX

Last updated: 2025-12-13

Overview

SpikeX (the “App”) provides market movement alerts and news headlines related to cryptocurrency markets. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App processes, how it is used, how it is shared, data retention practices, and how you can contact the developer.

1. Summary

  • The App does not require user account creation.
  • The App does not request or intentionally collect personal identifiers such as name, email address, or phone number.
  • The App may process network identifiers (such as IP address) as part of normal network communication and security operations.
  • The App fetches public news headlines via a developer-controlled proxy server.
  • The App stores a short-term local cache of recent headlines on the device to support offline viewing.

2. Information the App collects and processes

a) Data transmitted to developer-controlled server(s)

  • Request metadata: HTTPS requests to the news proxy endpoint (for example, query parameters such as days=3). Requests include standard HTTP headers (such as User-Agent and Accept), which may reveal general device and software information.
  • Network identifiers: The proxy server processes the client IP address as part of normal network communication and server logging. An IP address may be considered personal data under applicable data protection laws.

What is not collected: The App does not intentionally transmit advertising identifiers, precise location data, financial account information, authentication credentials, or contact details.

b) Data returned to the App

The proxy server returns public announcement data in JSON format. Typical fields include: id, title, url, publishedTsUtc, and optionally summary.

c) Local data stored on the device

  • Cached news and announcements stored locally for a short retention period (default: up to 3 days).
  • Local preferences (for example, UI or theme settings) stored using SharedPreferences. These do not contain personal identifiers.

d) Third-party libraries

The App uses standard open-source libraries (including Ktor, Jetpack Compose, and kotlinx.serialization) to provide core functionality. The App does not intentionally include analytics or advertising SDKs that collect personal data. If such SDKs are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

3. How information is used

  • To provide the App’s core functionality, including delivering market alerts and displaying news.
  • To operate and secure the proxy server (for example, logging, debugging, and abuse prevention).
  • To cache data locally and reduce repeated network requests.

4. Sharing and disclosure

  • The App does not sell personal data.
  • Data may be processed by infrastructure or hosting providers operating the proxy server on behalf of the developer, solely for providing and maintaining the service.
  • Information may be disclosed if required to comply with legal obligations or lawful requests by authorities.

5. Data retention

  • On-device cache: cached announcements are retained for a short period (default: up to 3 days).
  • Server logs: operational logs (which may include IP addresses) are retained for a limited period for security and abuse-prevention purposes (typically up to 30 days) and then deleted or rotated.

6. Security

  • Network communication uses HTTPS encryption.
  • Access to developer-controlled server data is restricted to authorized personnel.
  • No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

7. International data transfers

Server infrastructure may be located outside your country of residence. By using the App, you acknowledge that limited data (such as IP address and request metadata) may be processed in other jurisdictions.

8. Your rights and choices

  • You may revoke notification permissions at any time using system settings.
  • You may request deletion of data controlled by the developer by contacting the email address below.
  • Requests are typically handled within 30 days, subject to legal and operational requirements.

9. Disclaimer (Financial information)

SpikeX does not provide financial, investment, or trading advice. All alerts, signals, and information are provided for informational purposes only. You are solely responsible for your trading decisions and any resulting outcomes.

10. Children

The App is intended for adults (18+). It is not designed for children and does not knowingly collect personal information from minors.
